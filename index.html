Do's & Don'ts

Simple habits that make a powerful difference in reducing food waste.

✅

Do's

  • ✓Plan meals in advance to buy only what you need
  • ✓Store food properly to extend its shelf life
  • ✓Donate surplus food to NMC collection centres
  • ✓Use leftovers creatively in new meals
  • ✓Compost fruit, vegetable, and food scraps
  • ✓Check expiry dates before purchasing grocery items
  • ✓Serve smaller portions and take seconds if needed
❌

Don'ts

  • ✕Don't throw edible food directly into the bin
  • ✕Don't over-cook or over-order more than required
  • ✕Don't donate spoiled, expired, or rotten food
  • ✕Don't leave cooked food uncovered for too long
  • ✕Don't mix food waste with dry or hazardous waste
  • ✕Don't ignore food approaching its use-by date
  • ✕Don't waste packaged food without exploring donation
